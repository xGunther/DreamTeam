Music

A great movie always has a great soundtrack that sticks with you for years upon decades later. The Lion King is no exception to this, as it has many classics in their soundtrack list. The tracks mentioned on this page are part of the Original Motion Picture Sountrack released in 1994. Furthermore this music section will mention facts about every song in the film and will refer to links for these songs. Note that all of the songs on this soundtrack have been composed by Elton John, except for the scores, and are written by Tim Rice.

Circle of life

Circle of Life was composed by the famous Elton John and the lyrics were written by Tim Rice, who is an English lyricist and author. Furthermore the song was performed by Lebo Morake, who did the opening vocals in the Zulu language, and Carmen Twillie, who is the lead vocalist of the track. The song was nominated for both the Acadamy Award for Best Original Song and the Song of the Year Grammy in 1994, but did not win either of these awards.

Listen to "Circle of Life" on YouTube or on Spotify

I Just Can't Wait to Be King

I Just Can't Wait to Be King is performed by Jason Weaver, who is the singing voice of a young Simba. Rowan Atkinson, who is most famously known for his role of Mr. Bean, and Laura Williams provide suporting vocals in this track.

Listen to "I Just Can't Wait to Be King" on YouTube or on Spotify

Be Prepared

Be Prepared was performed by Jeremy Irons, who was the voice of Scar in the movie. Whoopi GoldBerg, Ceech Marin and Jim Cummings provided background vocals on this track.

Listen to "Be Prepared" on YouTube or on Spotify

Hakuna Matata

Hakuna Matata, perhaps the most famous song of the soundtrack, is performed by Nathan Lane and Ernie Sabella, who are the voice actors of Timon and Pumbaa respectively. Jason Weaver, who was mentioned ealier, is the voice of a young Simba in this track. As mentioned in the song, the phrase "Hakuna Matata" means no wories in Swahili.

Listen to "Hakuna Matata" on YouTube or on Spotify

Can You Feel the Love Tonight

Can You Feel the Love Tonight has many performers in the 1994 iteration of the movie, namely : Kristle Murden, Sally Dworsky, Joseph Williams, Nathan Lane and Ernie Sabella. Kristle Murden, Sally Dworsky and Joseph williams, who also is a member of the famous band Toto, are the lead vocalist on this track. This track won the Acadamy Award for Best Original Song and the Golden Globe Award for Best Original Song in 1995.

Listen to "Can You Feel the Love Tonight" on YouTube or on Spotify

Scores

All the scores in this move have been composed by the famous composer Hans Zimmer. A movie score is music that has been written to accompany a specific film. These scores are often instrumental only and the goal of them is to enhance a dramatic or emotional effect of a specific scene. In this section every score will be accompanied by a image during the scene of each score.

Elton John versions

These last three tracks of the soundtrack are songs that have been mentioned earlier, but this time they are performed by the British composer and singer Elton John. As mentioned earlier, more than half of the songs on this soundtrack have been composed by Elton himself. All of these three songs were nominated for the Acadamy Awards of Best Original Song and ultimately Can You Feel the Love Tonight won the award in 1995. Elton also won a grammy for the same song with the Best Male Pop Vocal Performance in 1995 Furthermore, he won 2 BMI Pop awards, a Golden Globe Award and an Ivor Novello Award for his contributions to the soundtrack.
